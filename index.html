Hi! I'm Mary Griffus, web developer, maker, and life-long learner. I've always felt learning and making go hand in hand. I love breaking down complex into primitive components and using that knowledge to make something greater than the sum of its parts. My love of emergent complexity has driven my hobbies, my career in teaching, and now my career as a web developer. Whether it is the design for a new app, a topic I am teaching, or a complex algorithm, I love to create.
